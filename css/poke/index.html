Articuno

Articuno is a dual-type Ice/Flying Legendary Pokémon introduced in Generation I.

Infernape

Infernape is a dual-type Fire/Fighting Pokémon introduced in Generation IV.

Rayquaza

Rayquaza is a dual-type Dragon/Flying Legendary Pokémon introduced in Generation III. While it is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon, Rayquaza can Mega Evolve into Mega Rayquaza if it knows Dragon Ascent, but only if it is not holding a Z-Crystal. Rayquaza is the game mascot of Pokémon Emerald.

Treecko

Treecko is a Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation III.

Lucario

Lucario is a dual-type Fighting/Steel Pokémon introduced in Generation IV.

Shaymin

Shaymin is a Grass-type Mythical Pokémon introduced in Generation IV. While it is not known to evolve into or from another Pokémon, Shaymin has a second Forme activated by using a Gracidea flower in the daytime.

Eevee

Eevee is a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I.

Luxray

Luxray is an Electric-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV.

Totodile

Totodile is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation II.

Mimikyu

Mimikyu is a dual-type Ghost/Fairy Pokémon introduced in Generation VII.

Darkrai

Darkrai is a Dark-type Mythical Pokémon introduced in Generation IV. It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon. It is a member of the Lunar duo with Cresselia.
